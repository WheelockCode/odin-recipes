Beef and Broccoli

This easy beef and broccoli recipe has an addictive sauce that's super quick to make from scratch! It rivals even the best takeout, yet it's way more affordable.

Ingredients

  • 1 Lbs - Flank Steak
  • 2 Tbsp - Olive Oil, divided
  • 3-4 Cups - Broccoli Florets

Sauce Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cups - Low-Sodium Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 Cups - Water
  • 1/2 Tsp - Apple Cider Vinegar or Rice Vinegar
  • 1/2 Tsp - Toasted Sesame Oil
  • 1/4 Cups - Brown Sugar, packed
  • 3 Tbsp - Cornstarch
  • 1 Tsp - Fresh Ginger, grated
  • 4 Cloves - Garlic, minced
  • 1/4 Tsp - Crushed Red Pepper Flakes, or more, to taste
